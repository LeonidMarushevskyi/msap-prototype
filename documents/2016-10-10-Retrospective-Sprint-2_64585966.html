Retrospective

What did we do well?

      • Was able to increase team velocity and close more story points compared to the previous sprint
      • Deployment engineers were able to setup infrastructure in Amazon

What should we have done better?

      • Was not able to finish responsive UI in the sprint. Need to be more careful on estimates in next sprint.
      • Some stories combine several features and can be separated into more granular stories.
      • This sprint we had a small imbalance between front-end development and back-end development due to vacation of one front-end developer. Front-end developers were not able to deliver enough artifacts for back-end development.

Actions

  • Leonid Marushevskiy - Make sure we have enough front end developer resources to maintain the right balance in next sprints
