Mississippi Agile Pool : 02 . Usability testing questions - foster parent

Created by Margreta Silverstone on Oct 10, 2016

 

Story:

You are a Foster Parent and would like to create a new account and search for a provider.

User Tasks:

Please access this URL: <insert appropriate URL> and perform the next three tasks and complete the survey. 

Task 1:

Complete the necessary steps to create a new account (entering in the required fields in the application).

Your Foster Care License is: ALE897C24

Task 2:

Your address is: 4411 Manhassett Dr, Jackson, MS 39211

The task is to find a provider for your 4 children.

You are a parent with 2 biological children (ages 5 and 7), and a Foster Parent of 2 children (ages 6 and 9). 

You need a provider for all 4 children after school, one day a week.

Use the search criteria that includes:

1. Cost information
2. Schedule information
3. Age categories
4. Parent reviews
5. Provider description

Results will list Providers who would meet your needs of after school care for 4 children within reasonable proximity.

Task 3:

From the search results, select a provider and create a message to your caseworker about funding.  Send message to the caseworker.   

Questions:

Were you able to perform task 3? (Yes/No)

Could you find the location(s) to put in the search information? (Yes/No)

Was the search criteria enough information to get wanted results? (Yes/No)

Were there advanced search filters for desired results? (Yes/No)

Could the search search results be sorted? (Yes/No)

Was navigating within the site easy? (Yes/No)

Was the system flexible to interact with? (Yes/No)

Could the system enhance the effectiveness/productivity of your job?  If yes, in what ways? (Free form)

Please rate on the scale 1 to 5 how easy is to perform the tasks (1 being easy and 5 not so easy)?

How long did it take to perform Task 3?  (Free form)

Any negative aspects of the system functionality? (Free form)

Key positive aspects of the system functionality? (Free form)

Overall, I am satisfied with the ease of completing the tasks in this scenario (agree through disagree scale)

Overall, I am satisfied with the amount of time it took to complete the tasks in this scenario (agree through disagree scale)

Overall, I am satisfied with the support information (online-line help, messages, documentation) when completing the tasks (agree through disagree scale)
