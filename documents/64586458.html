Mississippi Agile Pool : 03. Irina - October 11, 2016

Created by Pavel Khozhainov, last modified on Oct 12, 2016

Task 01:

You are  a parent of 4 years old boy. You plan to move to new area in Biloxi, Mississippi
Address is: 242 Lameuse St,  Biloxi, MS    
Please find a full Day Care facility closest to you.
Please also try to find Weekend Day Care in reasonable proximity.
 
-Irina’s comments:-

I experienced the following issues that prevented me from completing the task and liking the tool:

On the Home Page:

a)      Typo in text: Use filters below and "Search " button to fast searching child care providers. Should be: Use the filters below and "Search " button for fast search of child care providers
b)      The distance between the arrow incorrect 

c)       Unclear what does this information should tell me:

d)      Already mentioned that 24 months and 2 years options mean the same.
e)      It would be nice to have Clear All option in the age drop-down box.
f)       Incorrect alignment when Vietnamese is selected

g)      TAB does not work to move to the Age and Search controls – I am used to using keyboard for navigation.
2.       When I tried to hit search without logging in I was navigate to the Map page with the following popup. I was not logged in, so It did not make sense to me at that time.

When refreshing the page, the popup appears again.
When closing the popup, the address sets to: Jackson, Madison County, Mississippi, United States of America in the search field on the Map.
3.       Map page:
a)      When searching by address from the task, age = 4 yo and both Full Day Care AND Weekend Care – no results are returned. However, if I clear Weekend Care check box, the results are shown. Expected result: see facilities with either/both working schedules.
b)      I  cannot see where is what provider. Better show their names on the map or at least number them and show numbers.
c)       Map should also be centered to show the facility from top of my current sorted list and it should be maximized enough for me to see it.
d)      I cannot select to view a specific facility on the map.
e)      Filters area when expanded is taken half of my screen. As the filters are applied automatically, it’s difficult to track the results if I change something. I would prefer to have Apply button and the area to collapse once I click it.
f)       I  think it would be better to say “Show More Filters” instead of “Show All Filters”.
g)      It would be nice to see quality ratings as stars  - same as in facility information – in the All Quality Ratings filter.
h)      Filters for disabilities are very detailed, but it would be better to show them only when needed – after a user indicates that s/he looking for facility for children with special needs. Otherwise, those can be hidden and it will also give more meaning to them.
i)         “Put facility data here” field is confusing to me. What should I put there? The tooltip says I can put name, address or admin name there, but it works by the name only.
j)        Again, this is confusing. Better show Age Group and Quality Ratings captions.
k)      When switching to Vietnamese, the labels overlay.

l)        “Free Spots” is incorrect. Probably Available Slots were meant.
m)    Weird scrolling with empty space when filter area is opened

 n) If you provide some search criteria like provider name there is no way to execute search, only by pressing ENTER
